Who we are

We are working on gardening robots for solving the complex tasks of growing food.

Our final destination is to develope a multifuctional autonomus tool for the general public. Easy to use robot to help with the everyday tasks around your home.

PHASE I.

WEEDING ROBOT

Currently we are working with farmers who are facing changes with pesticies regulations. More an more reason to alternative ways to get rid of the unwanted passangers in their land. We working on the solution: A weeding robot. What can mechanicly distroy the weeds without the use of any chemical. Our test field focusing on sugar plantation, what is a popular product in our area.

We are big fan of everything can get us closer to a more sustainable future. In our development we are focusing on upcycling. We take a trown out hoverboards and make it into a functioning robot. What makes our costs low and our footprint even lower!
